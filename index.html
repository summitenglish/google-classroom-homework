Homework

1. Click on homework

2. Homework 'Assigned'

3. Click '+ Add or create'

4. Add homework photos/recordings

5. Click 'Hand in'

6. Homework 'Handed in'

7. Teacher checks homework and returns it back to student

8. Homework 'Returned'

10. Correct homework

11. DO NOT delete incorrect homework!!!

12. Click '+ Add or create'

13. Add corrected homework

14. Click 'Resubmit'

15. Homework 'Handed in'

16. Teacher checks if homework is finished
